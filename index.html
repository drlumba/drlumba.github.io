Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see a chiropractor?

No referral is required. You can book an appointment directly without seeing a physician first.

What can I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a detailed health history, discussion of your goals, a physical examination, and movement assessment. If appropriate, treatment may begin on the same visit.

Will I receive treatment on my first appointment?

In most cases, treatment begins on the first visit if it is safe and clinically appropriate.

What conditions do you commonly treat?

Common concerns include back pain, neck pain, joint pain, posture-related discomfort, sports-related injuries, and movement limitations.

Do you provide medical devices such as custom orthotics or compression stockings?

When clinically indicated, assessment and prescription of custom orthotics or compression stockings may be part of your care plan to support function and symptom management.

Custom Orthotics & Medical Devices

Custom Foot Orthotics

Custom foot orthotics are prescribed following a biomechanical and functional assessment. They support foot mechanics and may be used as part of a broader chiropractic care plan.

Many extended health benefit plans provide coverage for custom orthotics when prescribed by a regulated health professional. Coverage varies by insurer and plan.

Compression Stockings

Compression stockings may be recommended when clinically appropriate to support circulation and lower-limb comfort. Selection is based on individual assessment findings.

Some insurance plans include coverage for compression stockings when medical necessity criteria are met.

Insurance & Benefits Information

Coverage for medical devices depends on your individual benefits plan. Some insurers require documentation such as a prescription, diagnosis, or biomechanical assessment.

Receipts and supporting clinical documentation can be provided for insurance reimbursement where applicable. Direct billing availability varies by provider.
