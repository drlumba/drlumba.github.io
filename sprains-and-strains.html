Sprains & Strains

Sprains and strains are common musculoskeletal injuries affecting muscles, ligaments, and tendons. They often occur during sports, lifting, sudden movements, or repetitive daily activities.

Chiropractic care focuses on assessing movement, joint function, and tissue tolerance to help manage symptoms and support recovery.

Common Areas Affected

  • Neck and upper back
  • Low back
  • Shoulders and hips
  • Knees and ankles

Care Approach

  • Movement and functional assessment
  • Manual therapy when appropriate
  • Progressive mobility and strengthening exercises
  • Education for return to activity
