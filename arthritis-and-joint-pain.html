Arthritic & Degenerative Joint Pain

Arthritic joint pain is commonly associated with osteoarthritis and age-related changes in joint structure. Symptoms may include stiffness, reduced mobility, and discomfort during daily activities.

Chiropractic care emphasizes maintaining movement capacity, strength, and confidence in activity.

Commonly Affected Joints

  • Spine
  • Hips and knees
  • Shoulders

Care Approach

  • Joint and movement assessment
  • Exercise-based management strategies
  • Education on pacing and activity modification
