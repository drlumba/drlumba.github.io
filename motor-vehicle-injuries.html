Motor Vehicle Accident Injuries

Motor vehicle collisions can result in whiplash and soft tissue injuries that affect movement, confidence, and daily function.

Chiropractic care focuses on assessment, symptom management, and restoring movement capacity following a collision.

Common MVA Injuries

  • Whiplash-associated disorders
  • Neck and back pain
  • Shoulder and hip injuries

Care Considerations

  • Evidence-informed assessment
  • Gradual return to activity
  • Coordination with rehabilitation providers when required
